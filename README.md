## **_Getting Started_**

1. Press start
2. Enter each player's first name into the corresponding box
3. Player 1 (blue) goes first
4. On your turn:
   - Perform actions (optional)
   - Roll the dice to end your turn
   - Follow prompts for your roll
5. Take all your friend's money!
